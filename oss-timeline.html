Timeline: A History of Open Source in Government

This is a timeline of the major events, publications, and code releases in the history of the US Government's adoption of open source. If you like, you can contribute to the list of events in the form below.

This page uses a Google Spreadsheet as an event source for a Simile Timeline widget. It is based on the work of Kiyohito Yamamoto.
